Who I am

Why'd I come here, from the hustle and bustle of Kathmandu, a city of around 3 million people, to Grinnell, a sleepy town with less than nine thousand people? Unsurprisingly, I do get that question a lot but the answer is kinda complicated, so if you ever talk or text me, I'd be happy to recount. But in short, it was a combination of factors: scholarships, my uncle living nearby, moving away from home et cetera.
