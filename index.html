Who I am

I'm a Nepali Student studying in the USA

I am a Nepali national, born and raised in Kathmandu. I did my A-Levels from Budhanilkantha School, after which I took a gap-year and came to Iowa, United States, to study at Grinnell College. Why'd I come here, from the hustle and bustle of Kathmandu, a city of around 3 million people, to Grinnell, a sleepy town with less than nine thousand people? Unsurprisingly, I do get that question a lot but the answer is kinda complicated, so if you ever talk or text me, I'd be happy to recount. But in short, it was a combination of factors: scholarships, my uncle living nearby, developing independence away from home et cetera.

Other than my academic interests, I love learning and reading about history. I have certain phases every now and then when I go through every documentary, book or wikikpedia page there is about a certain historical topic. The topics are non-exhaustive, and it'd be too long to list them down ^_^
Other than that, I enjoy following the news, getting updated on current affairs, and reading about geo-politics :-) So if any one wants to talk to me about these stuffs, I'd be happy to engage in a convo!

I also play some Computer Games every once in a while. So if anyone is up for a game of Arma 3, Call of duty, let me know!
